A Look at Income and Inequality
in the United States

By Joshua Acosta and Terran King

In our project, we will aim to create a visualization on the income demographics and inequalities based in the US. The following data will represent incomes in the US based on race, gender, and age through different forms and interpretations of data from the US Census Population and Survey up to 2017.

The data displays the inequalities clearly. The reasons for these discrepencies, however, can be debated. Regardless of what conclusions we may come to for these inequalitites individually, we can notice the closing gaps and subtle trends in our incomes here in the United States.
